## On-Page Optimization Rules (derived from research)

### Homepage
- H1 must contain: **"Cửa Cuốn, Nhôm Kính, Cửa Kéo Cần Thơ"**
- Title tag: `Cửa Cuốn, Nhôm Kính, Cửa Kéo Cần Thơ | Hưng Thành Phát Door`
- First paragraph: mention Cần Thơ, Bình Thủy, 21 năm, bảo hành
- Include NAP (name, address, phone) in footer on every page

### Product Pages
- Each product subtype needs its own URL slug, e.g. `/cua-cuon-nhom-duc-can-tho`
- H1 pattern: `[Product Type] Cần Thơ — Hưng Thành Phát Door`
- Include: price range, warranty info, installation timeline, real project photos

### Service Pages to Create
- `/sua-cua-cuon-can-tho` — target "sửa cửa cuốn Cần Thơ" (250 vol, Diff 15)
- `/bao-gia-cua-cuon-can-tho` — target "báo giá cửa cuốn" (300 vol, Diff 18)
- `/cua-cuon-binh-thuy` — target "cửa cuốn Bình Thủy" (150 vol, Diff 10) ← easiest win

### Local SEO Signals
- Mention district names: Bình Thủy, Ninh Kiều, Cái Răng, Ô Môn, Thốt Nốt
- Use "gần đây" context via Google Business Profile posts (not on-page)
- Schema: LocalBusiness with geo coordinates already in layout.tsx ✓
